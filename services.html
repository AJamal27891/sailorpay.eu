Our Services

SailorPay offers a comprehensive suite of payment solutions designed to help your business navigate the complex world of online transactions.

We provide innovative payment processing services tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across various industries.

ACCOUNT SETUP

Merchant Account Setup

Our dedicated team simplifies the merchant account setup process, ensuring businesses can start accepting payments swiftly. We offer tailored solutions that cater to individual business requirements, making it easier than ever to get started with accepting electronic payments.

GATEWAY

Payment Gateway and Orchestration Platform

Our advanced PCI DSS payment gateway serves as the backbone of your payment processing infrastructure. It not only facilitates secure transactions but also seamlessly integrates with our payment orchestration platform.

GATEWAY DETAILS

Payment Gateway

Our payment gateway provides a secure and efficient way for businesses to accept a wide range of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, Ecommerce plugins and more. It ensures that your customers' transactions are processed smoothly and securely.

ORCHESTRATION

Payment Orchestration Platform

Enhance your payment processing efficiency with our cutting-edge payment orchestration platform. This intelligent system optimizes transaction routing, increasing approval rates and reducing declines. Real-time analytics give you valuable insights into your payment flows, enabling data-driven decision-making.

BANKING

Banking Services (Opening Bank Accounts)

Simplify your financial operations by utilizing our banking services. We assist businesses in opening bank accounts, a crucial step in managing your funds. Our team facilitates this process, helping you establish the necessary banking relationships required for seamless payment processing.
