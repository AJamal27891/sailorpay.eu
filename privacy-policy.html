Privacy Policy

Data Collected

We process your personal data, which falls under the following data categories:

  • Name/Company
  • Occupation/Job title
  • Date of birth
  • Company registration number
  • Contact person
  • Business address and other customer addresses
  • Contact details (telephone number, fax number, email address, etc.)
  • Bank connections, credit card data
  • Order data
  • UID number
  • Customer service requests

You have voluntarily provided us with data about yourself and we process this data on the basis of your consent for the following purposes:

  • Customer care and for our own advertising purposes, such as sending offers, advertising brochures and newsletters (in paper and electronic form), as well as for the purpose of indicating the existing or former business relationship with the customer (reference note).

You can revoke this consent at any time. A revocation means that we will no longer process your data for the above-mentioned purposes from this point on. To revoke, please contact Mag. Thomas Salomon at info@sailorpay.eu

The data provided by you is also required for contract fulfillment or for the implementation of pre-contractual measures. Without this data, we cannot conclude the contract with you.
